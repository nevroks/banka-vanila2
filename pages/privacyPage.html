How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We keep your information only so long as we need it to provide Services to you and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. This is also the case for anyone that we share your information with. When we no longer need to use your information and there is no need for us to keep it to comply with our legal or regulatory obligations, we’ll either remove it from our systems or anonymize it so that we can’t identify you.

We cannot, however, ensure or warrant the absolute security of any information you transmit to Gurtam or guarantee that your information on the Service may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by a breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards.

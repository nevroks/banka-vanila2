Our Journey

Gurtam started as a small business in 2002. Now it has grown into
a global leader in fleet management and IoT. Our software allows users
to monitor vehicle location, speed, fuel levels, engine performance, and
more in real time. And even more, could we imagine that one day our
products will help to save turtles' lives? No way!
